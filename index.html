Godwin Ponraj
PhD Student, Department of Biomedical Engineering, National University of Singapore.
I am a fourth year Ph.D student in the Department of Biomedical Engineering, National University of Singapore (NUS) under the supervision of Assoc. Prof. Hongliang Ren. Since 2016, I have been with the Medical Mechatronics lab, NUS, and have been working in the field of tactile sensing, control, and teleoperation of soft robotic systems. Before that, I received my Masters degree in Electrical Engineering with specialization in Automation and Control, from the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, NUS in 2016.

I am currently working on integrating origami and kirigami designs into close-range chip-less RF sensing for use in biomedical applications.
